Dubai Visa — Document Checklist

  • Clear copy of passport (including the external cover page)
  • Recent passport-size photo of the applicant
  • Return air ticket
  • Hotel voucher / accommodation proof
  • PAN card copy

Visa Duration: 30 Days or 60 Days  |  Validity: 60 Days

Processing: Normal — up to 4 working days  |  Express: 24 hours

We offer the following Dubai visa categories:

  • Dubai 48 Hrs Transit — Only via Emirates airline
  • Dubai 96 Hrs Transit — Only via Emirates airline
  • Dubai 30 Days — Single Entry & Multiple Entry
  • Dubai 60 Days — Single Entry & Multiple Entry

Contact us to find the category that best suits your travel plan and budget.

Oman Visa — Document Checklist

  • Valid passport
  • Recent passport-size photo of the applicant
  • PAN card copy

Visa Duration: 10 Days or 30 Days  |  Validity: 90 Days

Processing: Normal — 3 to 4 working days

Qatar Visa — Document Checklist

  • Coloured scanned copies of the first and last page of passport
  • Recent passport-size photo
  • Copy of PAN card

Visa Duration: 30 Days

Processing: Normal — up to 4 working days
